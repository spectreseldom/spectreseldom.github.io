Roles


Development Engineer

A development engineer is the person who does the work on the code of the project. At the moment, one of our team members know the language better than me, but I believe I am a valuable developer that can contribute ideas and make them come to life.


QA Engineer


The Quality Assurance Engineer takes a product and makes sure there are no bugs or otherwise faulty features with it. This role would suit me very well, because I can explore things in the game code and intentionally look for bugs, something I am very good at.


Project Manager


The project manager is responsible for all the steps in a products release and making. This role would not suit me well because I cannot be in charge of so much.


Product Manager


A Product Manager is someone who sees how useful the item will be in the market, and helps the product gain more traction, and helps the project progress itself, compared to a project manager, who makes the plan for the product. I would be better at this job because I would be able to work on the product a little bit.


Release Engineer


The release engineer makes sure everything is ready to launch before the product goes live. This would not suit me as I would need to make sure everything is ready, which is not my style, and other people on the team would be better at this.
