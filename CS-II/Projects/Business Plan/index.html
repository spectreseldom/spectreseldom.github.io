ISP Proposal

Partners: Jacob Fesseha, Soohan Cho

The Plan

Our plan is to make a game about geese. Similargames like the Untitled Goose Game and Deer Simulator, you will play as an animal (a goose) and run around causing wanton destruction. We will also allow online multiplayer, with the option to battle others.


How We Will Do It:

Using Roblox, we will make the game. Roblox is completely free to use and work with. Another option was to use Unity, but a problem was Roblox is easier to use and download, and Roblox has an in-built collaboration system. This will allow us to easily work together and share ideas.


Why Geese?

Similarly to the Deer Simulator, our goose game uses a possibly troublesome animal: Geese. Not only a big open world to explore, our game will also have bosses to fight and the opportunity to collaborate or fight the other people in the game.
