Why???

A digital portfolio is extremely useful to anyone going into any field, pretty much to everyone. A Digital Portfolio is similar to a resume in that it shows off your accomplishments on paper, but thats where the similarities end. Because a digital portfolio is online, it has infinite flexibility and is always being updated. Being able to show off everything you've done and accomplishments you've earned is useful for people wanting to know more about you. For those who work in digital art, computer software, or anything similar, a digital portfolio is invaluable in being hired and showing off your work.
