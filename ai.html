How Far, Exactly, Has AI Come?

AI is to the point where we could read an article and not realize it wasn't written by a human.
AI can generate photos that don't exist.
AI can pretend to be a human good enough to stump some, and that in itself will reap consequenses.

AI could be used in good ways.
AI could help solve algorithms and automate tasks humans do.
Yet, none of this is likely to come to pass.
AI will most likely be misused by someone for their own personal gain, and spread misinformation.
In todays society, many people on social media will be fooled by a chatbot that is spreading fake news.
Fake generated photos could support this, and professional articles are something no one will suspect.
We live in the age of information, but misinformation is just as easy to come by.
AI could be beneficial, but like humans, AI will fall short of greatness.
